
Homework goals
Learn to write and use simple regular expressions.


What needs to be done
Execute the task in the Main class in the calculateSalarySum method of the 08_Strings / TotalEarningsCalculatorReqExp project

Implement the receipt of the amount of earnings of each person from the text as a regular expression so that at the end of the program, 
the total amount of the earnings of people is calculated and printed. 
Any combination of names and the amount of salaries in the string can be passed to the method.


What is evaluated
Correctness of the program, regardless of the text passed to it as input.
